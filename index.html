Cardstock Clarity

Hi :). I'm a mindfulness practitioner who leverages introspective card readings and art therapy to guide discussions supporting your personal growth and mental healing. Each session is a dialogue—an invitation to slow down, reflect, and reconnect with the deeper layers of your own story. I use artistically expressive psychology, tarot and oracle cards as mindfulness tools for self-reflection rather than fortune-telling. These visual and symbolic systems help uncover patterns, emotions, and inner truths that may be influencing your path. Through these card's guided discussions, we aim to understand you and the transitions you’re moving through.

I use the tarot system because it is a deeply rich and thoughtfully structured framework that covers the entire spectrum of the human experience within 78 cards (growth, challenge, emotion, transformation, and renewal). This makes tarot a powerful tool for self-reflection, allowing us to explore our inner landscape from multiple perspectives. My readings are rooted in mindfulness, emotional awareness, and personal empowerment, helping you uncover meaning and guidance for your next steps.

Linda
Mindfulness Practitioner

Offerings

1:1 General Reading Session

30-60 minutes card reading for self-reflection or guidance. Explore current themes around your career, relationships, life direction, or personal growth. Sometimes reading may describe a potential future, depending on certain conditions changing or remaining the same; but it represents just one of many possible outcomes shaped by your choices and free will. Overall, my readings are reflective—not predictive—and aim to empower your awareness and decision-making.

1:1 Metaphoric Associative Cards Session

60-90+ minutes Only available for returning querents. The session begins with a brief meditation to relax the mind and bring the subconscious closer to the surface. From there, we explore images that invite your inner self to express its story, revealing underlying emotions and themes. Together, we uncover what’s seeking expression and close by tuning into your intuition for guidance. Note: MAC sessions are reflective and exploratory in nature and may intense emotions or memories, session close with a healing oracle reading to restore balance and grounding.

1:1 Inner Child (Shadow) Session

60-150+ minutes. Only available by invitation for returning querents since each session is personalized based on my ongoing knowledge your patterns. These session are about exploration of the hidden emotions and patterns shaping your experience. By bringing these aspects into awareness with compassion, you create space for healing, integration, and renewed clarity. Please note: Shadow work may surface past wounds or repressed memories; sessions conclude with uplifting/healing oracle reading.

SENSITIVE CONTENT WARNING: Please feel free to let me know if there are any specific types of imagery you do not want to see. Some of my decks include symbolic or literal depictions of nudity, sexuality, violence, or mental health struggles such as self-harm but can be excluded as requested.

DISCLAIMER: I like to use card readings as tools for self-awareness, introspection, and healing support. They may complement personal growth, but they are not a replacement for therapy or professional mental health care.
