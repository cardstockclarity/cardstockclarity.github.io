About

I’m a mindfulness practitioner blending expressive psychology cards, tarot, and oracle decks as tools for self-reflection (not fortune-telling). These visual systems help surface emotions, patterns, and truths shaping your path. Together we use guided discussion and simple creative exercises to translate insight into next steps.

Tarot offers a rich, structured language for the full human experience—growth, challenge, transformation, and renewal. My approach is practical, compassionate, and empowerment-focused.

Linda Lam
Mindfulness & Card Guide
