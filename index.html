About

I am a mindfulness practitioner who leverages introspective card readings and art therapy to guide discussions to aid in your personal growth and mental health. Each session is a dialogue—an invitation to slow down, reflect, and reconnect with the deeper layers of your own story. I use artistically expressive psychology cards, as well as tarot and oracle cards as mindfulness tools for self-reflection rather than fortune-telling. These visual and symbolic systems help uncover patterns, emotions, and inner truths that may be influencing your path. Through these card's guided discussions, we aim to understand you and the transitions you’re moving through.

I use the tarot system because it is a deeply rich and thoughtfully structured framework that covers the entire spectrum of the human experience within 78 cards (growth, challenge, emotion, transformation, and renewal). This makes tarot a powerful tool for self-reflection, allowing us to explore our inner landscape from multiple perspectives. My readings are rooted in mindfulness, emotional awareness, and personal empowerment, helping you uncover meaning and guidance for your next steps.
